"Pool Hall" Memberships

Cousins Snooker and Pool venues operate on a membership system. Memberships can be obtained by visiting either club, anytime during our opening hours. To register for a membership, customers are required to bring each of the following documents.

  • Photographic ID (e.g. Passport or Driving licence)
  • Proof of address (e.g. Utility bill)

Membership is charged as a one-time payment of £55 - for life

Members are free to visit both our Holloway and Edmonton clubs, there is no need for separate memberships for each club.

Members are allowed to bring up to a maximum of 5 guests at a time, each guest will be required to pay a guest fee of £5.
